Howdy Owls

The owls you need to have

The owls are the result of the first collaborative project between StokkeAustad and Andreas Engesvik, Oslo. They were originally made for the exhibition Designblok in Prague. The Owls were modelled in polystyrene and then milled out of solid wood.
